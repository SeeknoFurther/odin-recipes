Description

Georgie's go-to recipe for divine days by the fire. Hearty, and exceedingly satisfying

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs good olive oil
  • 8 ounces bacon, diced
  • 2 1/2 lbs beef chuck cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 1 lb carrots, sliced diagonally into 1-inch chunks
  • 2 yellow-onions, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic (2 cloves)
  • 1/2 cup Cognac or good brandy
  • 1 750ml bottle dry red wine such as Burgundy
  • 2 to 2 1/2 cups canned beef broth
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 4 tbs (1/2 stick) unsalted butter at room temperature, divided
  • tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 lb frozen small onions
  • 1 lb mushrooms, stems discarded, caps thickly sliced
For Serving
  • Country bread toasted or grilled
  • 1 garlic clove, cut in half
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh, flat-leaf parsley (optional)
